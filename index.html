Our Journey

How a simple idea blossomed into a thriving community.

The Spark (2022)

Kendy and Sally, long-time friends, share their dream of creating a more positive and connected online world over a cup of coffee. The initial concept is born.

First Lines of Code (Early 2023)

The vision starts to take shape. The first prototype is built, focusing on a clean, calming interface and core features for meaningful interaction.

Beta Launch (Mid 2023)

We invite a small group of founding members to test the platform. Their invaluable feedback helps refine the experience and solidify our community values.

Public Opening (2024)

Kendy & Sally officially opens to the public, ready to welcome everyone seeking connection, inspiration, and a place to belong.
